  • Rival Sons is a rock band known for their powerful sound and vintage-inspired style. The group was formed in Long Beach, California, in 2009 and consists of vocalist Jay Buchanan, guitarist Scott Holiday, bassist Dave Beste, and drummer Mike Miley.

    Rival Sons draws heavily from classic rock influences, incorporating elements of blues, soul, and hard rock into their music. Their sound has often been compared to iconic bands like Led Zeppelin and The Rolling Stones. With their energetic performances and raw, soulful vocals, Rival Sons has gained a reputation for their captivating live shows.

    Rival Sons' music often tackles social and political themes, reflecting their desire to make a meaningful impact with their lyrics. Songs like "Pressure and Time" and "Electric Man" showcase their ability to deliver powerful messages while maintaining a catchy and dynamic sound.

    Over the years, Rival Sons has toured extensively, sharing the stage with notable acts such as Black Sabbath and Deep Purple. Their reputation as a formidable live band has earned them a dedicated fan base around the world.
